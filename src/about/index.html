About OnlineReality.xyz

OnlineReality.xyz is a game platform built with the premisse of Joining the Two Worlds. Currently the website has about 5 to 10 games played per day from people all around the world!

The Online Reality project started within the scope of the LSIS2 (Systems Labs II) class, a curricular unity of the Systems Engineering bachelor.

Our platform has, as its main goals, the FIGHT against self-isolation, depression and anti-social behaviour that has been increasing, more and more, due to the COVID19 pandemic that has been deteriorating our mental, physical and social health.

Goals

  1. Provide a fun and healthy place for people to socialize together
  2. Fight the depression caused by isolation
  3. Make socializing fun and engaging
  4. Decrease the monotony of remote work
  5. Improve the social skills of the user

We still have a long way to go in achieving these goals since this is only a prototype, but we are well aware about the positive impact we can have on peoples lives with our webgame and, therefore, that exact impact will always be our main priority and concern.
